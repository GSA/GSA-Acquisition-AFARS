5150.103-5 Processing cases.

(1) Within five working days of receipt of a request for contract adjustment, regardless of dollar amount, the contracting officer shall send a copy, through procurement channels (See AFARS 5101.290 Routing documents and mailing addresses.(b)(2)(ii)(B)), to the following address:

Attn: SAGC, Recorder, Army Contract Adjustment Board

Office of the General Counsel

104 Army Pentagon

Washington, DC 20310-0104.

(2) Send documentation to the addressee in paragraph (1) of this section.
