5117.502-2 The Economy Act.

(a) Assisted acquisitions that are subject to the Economy Act shall comply with:

(i) DoD Financial Management Regulation DoD 7000.14-R, Vol 11A Chapter 3 and

(ii) DFAS-IN Regulation 37-1, Chapter 12.

(c) The requiring activity is responsible for preparing the Economy Act determination and findings (D&F). Approval of the D&F is in accordance with DFAS-IN Regulation 37-1, Chapter 12. The Economy Act D&F is required for both direct and assisted acquisitions, in addition to the approval to use a non-DoD contract, when applicable (see 5117.770).
