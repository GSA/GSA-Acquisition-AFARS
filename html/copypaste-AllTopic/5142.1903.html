5142.190-3 Reporting.

Each contracting or contract administrative office shall submit the Status Report on Specified Contract Audit Reports (RCS DD-IG (SA)1580), as required by DoDI 7640.2 to reach the addressee at 5101.290(b)(2)(ii)(A), not later than 30 November and 31 May. Obtain the program and instructions from the addressee at 5101.290(b)(2)(ii)(A). Negative reports are required.

Parent topic: 5142.190 Follow-up on contract audit reports.
