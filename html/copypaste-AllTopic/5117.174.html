5117.174 Multi–year contracts for electricity from renewable energy sources.

(a) The head of the contracting activity shall perform the functions as described in DFARS 217.174(a). See Appendix GG for further delegation.

(b) Limitations. The head of the contracting activity may exercise the authority as stated in DFARS 217.174(b). See Appendix GG for further delegation.
