5133.190-1 Bid protest action report.

Within 15 calendar days following notification of resolution of a GAO protest, the contracting officer must send a bid protest action report in the format in 5153.303-6 (double spaced between item numbers) to the following addressees:

(a) AMC contracting activities send the report to the addressee in 5101.290(b)(7).

(b) USACE contracting activities send the report to

U.S. Army Corps of Engineers

Attn: CECC-C

441 G St., N.W.

Washington, DC 20314-1000.

(c) All other contracting activities send the report to the addressee in 5133.104(a)(3)(i)(1)(iii).
